Our story
10
years of flying in and out of Udaipur

We got tired of bad cab experiences. So we built a better one.

Udaipur is our home. For a decade, we dealt with the same frustrations every traveller does. Drivers negotiating fares mid-booking, unclean cars, someone glued to their phone the whole ride.

You are already exhausted from travelling. The last thing you need is that. So we built the experience we always wanted: reliable cabs, fixed prices, drivers who actually take care of you. That is Aavre.
