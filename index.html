Good to know

Questions, answered.

How much is a cab from Udaipur airport?

Airport transfers within the city are a flat ₹499 for any point within 35 km. Outstation transfers are fixed too, for example ₹1,200 to Nathdwara and ₹2,000 to Kumbhalgarh. The fare is agreed before your ride, with no surge and no negotiation.

How do I book a cab with Aavre?

You book directly with us on WhatsApp. Send your pickup point, destination, date and number of guests, and we confirm your ride and the fixed fare before you travel.

Do you charge extra at night or during peak season?

No. Your fare is fixed and the same at any hour and any time of year. There is no night surcharge and no seasonal surge pricing.

How many people can the cab seat?

Each cab sits up to 4 guests with room for luggage. With a full car we comfortably fit two medium check-in bags, one cabin bag and a couple of backpacks.

Do you offer day trips from Udaipur?

Yes. We run fixed fare trips to Kumbhalgarh, Chittorgarh, Ranakpur and Nathdwara, along with half day and full day hire for sightseeing at your own pace.

Are your cabs safe?

Yes. Every cab is 5 star safety rated with 6 airbags, a dashcam and GPS on every ride, so you are looked after from pickup to drop.

Are your cabs electric?

Yes. Our fleet is fully electric, which keeps every ride quiet, smooth and clean.
